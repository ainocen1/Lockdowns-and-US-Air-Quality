This folder contains daily area-weighted average values of Aerosol Optical Depth (AOD) at the census tract level for the contiguous US, for the December to May periods from 2015 to 2022.

AOD is a unitless measure of tiny solid and liquid particulates, which includes smoke, dust, and particulate matter, where higher levels are associated with higher air pollution.
