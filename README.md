# Lockdowns-and-US-Air-Quality
This contains daily air quality (AOD and PM2.5) and weather data aggregated at the census tract level for the contiguous US, December to May periods of 2015 to 2022. These correspond to the data used in "Clearing the Air, Unequally: Disparities in the Long-Term Air Quality Impacts of COVID-19 Lockdowns in the U.S." (dissertation chapter version).
